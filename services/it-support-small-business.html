Common questions

Small business IT support, explained.

What kind of IT support does a small business actually need?

Usually not enterprise-style help desk and network administration. Most owner-led businesses need help choosing and connecting software, keeping workflows and dashboards working, and having someone to ask before buying a new tool.

Is this the same as hiring an IT managed service provider (MSP)?

Not exactly. A traditional MSP focuses on hardware, networks, and security tickets. Bromsey Systems focuses on the software and workflow layer — CRMs, automations, dashboards, and the systems your team actually works in every day.

Can IT support be combined with automation and custom software?

Yes — this is usually delivered as part of an ongoing Systems Partner retainer, which includes software recommendations, automation backlog management, and dashboard improvements together.

How much does small business IT support cost?

Ongoing IT and systems support is typically scoped as a $2,500–$7,500 per month Systems Partner retainer, sized to how many tools and workflows your business runs on.
