Common questions

AI workflow consulting, without the hype.

What does an AI workflow consultant actually build?

Usually practical, narrow tools: drafting customer replies from an inbox, summarizing intake forms or documents, extracting data from PDFs or emails into your CRM, or answering internal questions from your own documentation. Not a general-purpose chatbot with no clear job.

Is AI workflow consulting only useful for tech companies?

No. The most useful AI workflows for small businesses are usually document, email, and intake processing — things field services, professional services, and other owner-led businesses deal with every day.

How do you avoid AI hype and pick something that actually works?

We start from the manual task, not the technology. If a repetitive task can be reliably handled with a simple automation, we use that. AI gets used specifically where judgment, summarization, or drafting is genuinely needed.

How much does AI workflow consulting cost?

AI-assisted workflows are usually scoped as part of a Workflow Sprint ($2,000–$8,000) or a Custom Micro App ($10,000-plus) depending on complexity. A Workflow Audit ($1,500–$3,500) determines which applies.
