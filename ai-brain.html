How it works

You set the triggers. The brain does the rest.

This is not a one-time export into a folder. It is a pipeline that keeps running.

You decide what counts as new intake — a ChatGPT thread wrapping up, a Claude chat ending, a new email landing, a call note getting logged. Whatever trigger you set, it gets scraped, formatted, and written back to the brain automatically.

Nothing sits unindexed for long. What you learned this morning is queryable by the afternoon.

FAQ

Common questions.

If it's not covered here, ask us directly on the intake form.

Is my data secure?

Your data stays in your own accounts and infrastructure wherever possible. We connect to sources with least-privilege access and document exactly what is indexed.

Which tools do you connect to?

Common sources are email, CRM, shared docs, databases, and content platforms. If your team uses something specific, we scope for it during setup.

Do I need clean data first?

No. Part of the build is formatting and structuring intake as it comes in — that is what the nightly re-index step is for.

Can I start with just one use case?

Yes. Most companies start with two or three — onboarding and pricing are common first picks — then expand once the brain is live.
